Data Science Specialization

Johns Hopkins University has put together a series of nine courses related to data science. In addition to these foundational courses, this specialization offered the opportunity to do a capstone project upon competition of the courses. At the current time, I have completed the foundational courses. The capstone project has not been offered yet, but I look forward to it once it is available.
