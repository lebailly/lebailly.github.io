Additional Remarks (not mentioned in the syllabus)

 

Homework Due Dates: 

 

Homework will typically be due a week after we go over the material in class.  For example, if we cover section 6.1 on a Wednesday, the homework for that section will be due the following Tuesday at 11:59 PM.  This will not work, however, for the last week of class.  I will let you have sometime after the final exam to finish the last homeworks (though it will be to your advantage to finish them before the final).  The specific details of what assignments are due when will be posted on WebAssign.

 

Material on Quizzes:

 

A quiz will not cover material that we go over the day of the quiz.  Thus you will know by the end of Wednesday what sections will be on the quiz.

 

Exam Makeup Policy: 

 

Quizzes cannot be made up.  Do remember that your lowest quiz score is dropped, so if you miss one that will be the one that is dropped.  If you have a legitimate reason for not taking an exam at the scheduled time you make it up within a reasonable time frame.  It is my discretion to determine what is a legitimate reason and what is a reasonable time frame.  Please see me as soon as possible if you realize you will not be able to make an exam.

 

Calculator Policy:

 

None of the quiz or exam questions will require a calculator. You will be allowed to use a scientific calculator on quizzes and exams, but it will not be necessary to do the problems.  Graphing calculators are not allowed.

 

Do note that some homework problems occasional will require the aid of a calculator (if you do not have one Google should suffice).
