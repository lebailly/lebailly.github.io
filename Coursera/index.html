BMI and Diabities Risk Score Calculator

A Shiny Web App

Chris LeBailly
Coursera Data Science Specilizatoin

Purpose

  • Type 2 diabities is on the rise, from around 30 million people diagnosed in 1985 to 285 million in 2010.

  • A number of lifestyle factors are known to be important to the development of type 2 diabetes

  • Early detection of lifestyle factors that put a person at risk can help a person make lifestyle changes

  • The objective of this shiny web app is to make it easy for people to understand their diabities risk factors.

The User Interface

The user enters the following numeric information.

  • Age
  • Height (inches)
  • Weight (pounds)

The user selects (via radio buttons) their:

  • Gender
  • Types of Medications currently taking
  • Family history of diabetes
  • Smoking history

The app responds imdeiately to input so users can see how lifestyle changes affects their risk (i.e. if they quit smoking). Their Body Mass Index and Diabities Risk factor are outputed.

Methodology

This applicatoin is based off of this diabetes risk factor calcuator.

In this app, given the users weight (\(w\)) in pounds and height (\(h\)) in inches, we compute the user's Body Mass Index (\(BMI\)) using

\[BMI = \frac{703 \cdot w}{h^2} \]

To compute the diabetes risk factor (\(R\)) we need to know the persons sex (\(S\)), if they are taking hypertension medication (\(Rx_{HTN}\)), if they are taking steriod medications (\(Rx_{STR}\)), their age (\(Age\)), their BMI risk (\(BMI_r\)), if a parent or sibling has type 2 diabetes (\(FHM\)), and if they smoke or used to smoke (\(Smk\)). The BMI risk is determed based off of \(BMI\), which this app computes.

\[R = \frac{100}{1+e^\theta}\] \[\theta = 6.322 - S - Rx_{HTN} - Rx_{STR} - 0.063*Age - BMI_r - FHM - Smk\]
